Help & FAQ
What does this extension track?
It records your active tab usage time by domain, and lets you view stats, ignore certain sites, or set daily limits.

What does "Data Retention" mean?
This controls how many days of tab history are stored. Old data is deleted automatically.

How do I ignore a site?
Add each domain (e.g. youtube.com) on a new line in the Ignore List and click Save.

What does "Time Limits" do?
Set daily minute limits for any domain. Youâ€™ll get a notification if you exceed your limit.

How do I export/import data?
Use Export to download your full history/settings as a JSON file, or Import to restore from a JSON file.
Use "Export Filtered Data" to download only the visible part of your top sites table as CSV.

What does "Reset to Defaults" do?
It restores your settings (ignore list, limits, retention, etc) to their defaults. Your usage history is NOT deleted.
